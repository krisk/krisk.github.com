A special message to The Obvious Corporation

Pardon the novel, but I need more than 140 characters.

Let me just go ahead and say that I love my current position, and employer. Frankly, they really do not give me any reason to leave. Nonetheless, I wouldn’t say that the technology I create helps people on a personal and social level (ex, twitter, blogger, or other collaborative web systems), though, it definitely does help them make money. However, is that really the ONLY type of technology that I want to keep creating? Do my projects have to exist solely for the enjoyment of a very few privileged individuals? I’m not quite sure.

However, in working with the Nerd of the Year, the failed astrophysicist, and Evan (no meat? Really? See my postscript below) I hope to create technology whose success is directly proportional to the positive impact it has, and, if I may kindly steal from your website, technology that makes people happy. That’s probably my biggest motivation.

All right, I concede. The technology should definitely be financially rewarding.

That's all for now. Contact me for any questions or comments.
